Refund & Cancellation

Last updated: January 2025 · Diamond Touch Cleaning Ltd
We want every clean to be perfect. If something isn't right, we'll always make it right — that's our promise to you.

1. Overview

Our cancellation and refund policy is designed to be fair to both our clients and our team. Please read this policy before booking.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • More than 24 hours before service: Full cancellation, no charge.
  • Less than 24 hours before service: A cancellation fee of 50% of the quoted service price may apply.
  • Same-day cancellation or no-show: Full service charge may apply to cover our team's time and travel.

3. Rescheduling

You may reschedule your booking free of charge with at least 24 hours' notice. Rescheduling with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a small admin fee.

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. If you are dissatisfied with a completed clean, we will first attempt to resolve the issue by returning to reclean the affected area at no charge. Monetary refunds are issued at our discretion where a reclean is not possible or appropriate.

5. Our Re-Clean Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with the standard of cleaning, contact us within 24 hours of the service. We will return within 48 hours to address any issues, at absolutely no extra cost to you.

6. How to Cancel or Reschedule

To cancel or reschedule, please contact us by phone, WhatsApp, or email as soon as possible. We kindly ask for as much notice as you can give.
